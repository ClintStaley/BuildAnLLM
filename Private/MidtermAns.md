It does make a decent interview question for LLM researchers though — "why did early GPT models struggle to count the letters in a word" separates people who understand the architecture from people who've just used the API.
You could make it harder by asking why the problem is worse for some words than others — which requires knowing that common words tend to be single tokens while unusual words get fragmented, so the model's spelling ability is inversely correlated with word frequency in an interesting way.
